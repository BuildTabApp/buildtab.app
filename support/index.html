Frequently asked questions

When adding an expense, you enter the total amount you purchased (e.g. 1kg of filament for $25) and how much you actually used on this project (e.g. 200g). BuildTab automatically calculates the cost: 200g ÷ 1000g × $25 = $5.00.

This way you only track what you actually spent on each project, not the full price of materials you'll use across multiple builds.
